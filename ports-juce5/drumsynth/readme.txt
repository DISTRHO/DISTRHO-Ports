Information-------------------------------------------------------------

Drumsynth is a JUCEified port of the excellent Paul Kellet's DrumSynth.
Features:
- 32 voices polyphonic
- 24 drum notes (2 octaves mapping)
- 1 tone with sweep
- 2 overtones
- 3 noize generators
- envelope editor
- midi automatable with learn function
- import/export of DS files
